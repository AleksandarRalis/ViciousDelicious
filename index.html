About Us

Welcome to our music band! We are a passionate group of two different individuals dedicated to bringing you the best live performances.

Vicious Delicious was born from a spark that mixed attitude, melody, and a taste for the unexpected. Our name came from a joke during an early rehearsal—someone said our sound felt “vicious in energy but delicious to the ears,” and it stuck instantly. Two years later, that spirit still drives us. We blend pop‑rock world hits with the unmistakable flavor of Macedonian pop‑rok, creating a live atmosphere that feels both familiar and fiercely our own. Every show is a celebration of rhythm, nostalgia, and raw fun, powered by a band that lives for the stage and the crowd that keeps coming back for more.
